The Best Banana Bread Recipe!

by Jaeyoon Kim

Description

This banana bread is partially copied from an online recipe but is mostly gibberish

Enjoy!

Collect the following ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Ripe bananas
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Egg

Steps

Follow these instructions carefully

  1. Mix the flour, water, egg, and sugar
    • Mix until there are no clumps
    • If you don't know what no clumps looks like, eye doctors can be found here
  2. Add the baking soda and salt
  3. Peel your ripe bananas and mush until smooth
  4. Add bananas and remaining ingredients
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees C
  6. Bake for around 40 minutes, or until fully cooked through
  7. Enjoy!
